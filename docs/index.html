FormulaForge

FormulaForge offers a fresh take on editing and styling mathematical formulas motivated by a simple insight: it's better to edit content via markup, but so much simpler to edit styles with a point-and-click interface.

So it gives you exactly that: just paste in your LaTeX code and you'll see it instantly rendered in the canvas. Styling is a breeze with intuitive selections and menus of all of the customizations you need.

A Hot Take on Formulas

Visual editing

Select elements of your formulas by simply clicking on them. Customize your formula with colors, boxes, annotations, and more. No more sifting through piles of LaTeX just to color one symbol!

Code sync

As you customize your formula, the code pane updates to show you exactly how the underlying LaTeX changed. Noticed an error in your math? Just edit the code! Your customizations will stay.

Live updates

Waiting for LaTeX to recompile your whole document is a thing of the past. Stay in flow with instant visual updates as you customize your formulas, even as you edit the LaTeX code.
